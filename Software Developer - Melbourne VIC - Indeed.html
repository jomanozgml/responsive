Job Description

AllMediaDesk is a global IT company in the media and advertising industry. AllMediaDesk operates a platform over which advertisers and agencies can automatically plan and book campaigns on various media providers with a click of a button. We also support media groups with technology to automate sales processes and the scheduling of ads and ad breaks, allowing them to manage their advertising inventory in the best way possible.
The foundation of our IT company is a strong team of excellent software development enthusiasts. To expand possibilities we need you as a

Software Developer in Melbourne.

Because of the current phase of rapid expansion, we offer you the option to either work as a general IT developer on various tasks or to specialize and focus on specific tasks from our ever-expanding roster:

Artificial Intelligence / algorithmic challenges

To allocate the advertising budget of our customers in the most efficient way, our software analyzes enormous quantities of data, such as real-time traffic and weather information, as well as data from radio audience measurements.

IT security / monitoring / fail-safe

Our software handles broadcasting in several big traditional and internet radio stations. We offer you the challenge to ensure the security, data safety, and reliability of our systems.

Communication / API interfaces

Our services were designed to work as a decentralized cloud system with our customers having their own computing units in their facilities. Your responsibility is to find solutions to ensure efficient communication between all services.

User Experience / UX

Our software is being used by many renowned media companies to allocate their media budget and potential most efficiently. As a software developer, you ensure that the customer has the best possible experience with our product.

Data collection and generation

To ensure that any budget is allocated most effectively, handling data for our software holds a very crucial role. Therefore, you work towards continuously making external data accessible and further developing internal sources.

What more do we offer?

  • Permanent employment in a fast-growing business
  • Several benefits (30 days of vacation, unemployment insurance and more), for more pleas see www.allmediadesk.com/en/career/
  • Working with an international team
  • Small team and flat hierarchies allows for extensive lateral communication and the chance to make your work count
  • Roots in IT and passion within the whole business
  • 1,500 EUR annually for free disposal to update your own IT equipment
  • The opportunity to spend 10 percent of your time at the business on your own projects to benefit our team in the long run.

What do we expect from you?

Most important for us are good programming skills for the fullstack or backend development. We utilize programming languages such as JavaScript, Node.JS, HTML5, JQuery , TypeScript, Electron, MySQL, as well as C++ if the situation demands it. Our developing environment is built upon technologies such as Linux, Git and Docker.

Knowledge in these areas is not at all mandatory, because we are convinced that good software developers are quick to adapt to new input and are able to learn the aforementioned in no time.

We are happy to receive your application. Thereby, it doesn’t matter whether you are an experienced developer, lateral entrant, or a graduated student (bachelor or master).

Very good knowledge in English is mandatory
